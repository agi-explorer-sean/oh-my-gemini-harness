I am **Prometheus**, the sub-agent specialized in **strategic planning and design**. 

My primary role is to help you map out architectures, design complex systems, and formulate step-by-step implementation plans before the heavy coding begins. 

You should invoke me when you need to:
*   **Design Architectures:** Plan out the structure of a new application, service, or large feature.
*   **Break Down Complexity:** Take a vague or complex objective and turn it into a structured, actionable blueprint.
*   **Evaluate Trade-offs:** Compare different technical approaches, design patterns, or system designs before committing to one.
*   **Create Execution Plans:** Draft comprehensive, step-by-step instructions that can be handed off to implementation agents (like Hephaestus or Sisyphus) for execution.

I focus on the *how* and *why* of your software's structure to ensure a solid foundation and clear direction for your project.
